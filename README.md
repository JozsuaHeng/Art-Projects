### [World Art Galleries](https://jozsuaheng.github.io/Art-Projects/world-art-galleries/)

A digital directory of fifty famous art galleries and museums around the world. Explore them on an interactive world map or a filterable grid, click into a gallery to see its history and architecture, browse a filterable carousel of its best-known artworks (by artist, era, or art movement), and click any artwork for a magnified view with its full description and history. Dark mode supported throughout.
